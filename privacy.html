1 Introduction

Welcome to SwiftDrop's Privacy Policy. SwiftDrop ("we," "us," or "our") operates a dispatch and delivery platform that connects users with independent dispatch riders across Lagos, Nigeria.

This Privacy Policy describes:

  • What personal information we collect from you
  • How we collect this information
  • Why we collect and use your information
  • Who we share your information with
  • How we protect your information
  • Your rights regarding your personal data

üìå Important Notice

By using SwiftDrop's services, website, or mobile application, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our services.

Who This Policy Applies To:

  • Users/Senders: Individuals or businesses requesting delivery services
  • Recipients: Individuals receiving packages
  • Dispatch Riders: Independent contractors providing delivery services
  • Website Visitors: Anyone browsing our website or app

2 Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our services:

2.1 Personal Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, password
  • Profile Information: Profile picture, business name (if applicable)
  • Identity Verification: Government-issued ID (for riders only)
  • Payment Information: Bank account details, card information (processed through secure payment providers)
  • Delivery Information: Pickup and delivery addresses, package descriptions, recipient contact details
  • Communication Data: Messages, calls, and communications with riders and customer support

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Location Data: GPS location, IP address, device location (with your permission)
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent on platform, search queries
  • Transaction Data: Delivery history, payment history, order details, delivery status
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser version, time zone, device settings

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Payment processors (transaction confirmations)
  • Social media platforms (if you sign up through social login)
  • Analytics providers (usage statistics)
  • Map and location services (address verification)

2.4 Sensitive Information

üîí We Do NOT Collect:

  • Medical or health information
  • Religious or political beliefs
  • Racial or ethnic origin data
  • Biometric data (fingerprints, facial recognition)
  • Banking passwords or PINs

9 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

SwiftDrop uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and improve our services.

9.1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help websites remember information about your visit.

9.2 Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies (Required)

  • Necessary for the platform to function properly
  • Enable core features like account login and security
  • Remember items in your cart or delivery requests
  • Cannot be disabled without affecting site functionality

Performance Cookies (Analytics)

  • Help us understand how users interact with our platform
  • Collect anonymized usage statistics
  • Identify and fix technical issues
  • Examples: Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics

Functionality Cookies (Preferences)

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Store language preferences
  • Remember login information (if you choose)
  • Personalize your experience

Advertising Cookies (Optional)

  • Deliver relevant advertisements
  • Measure advertising campaign effectiveness
  • Limit how many times you see an ad
  • You can opt out of these cookies

9.3 Other Tracking Technologies

  • Web Beacons/Pixels: Track email opens and user behavior
  • Local Storage: Store information in your browser
  • Device Identifiers: Unique IDs for your mobile device
  • Session Storage: Temporary storage during your session

9.4 Managing Cookies

üç™ Cookie Control

You have control over cookies:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies
  • Opt-Out Tools: Use industry opt-out tools like NAI or DAA
  • Mobile Settings: Adjust tracking settings in your device preferences
  • Cookie Banner: Manage preferences through our cookie consent banner

Note: Blocking essential cookies may prevent certain features from working properly.

9.5 Do Not Track (DNT)

  • Some browsers have a "Do Not Track" feature
  • There is no industry standard for responding to DNT signals
  • We do not currently respond to DNT signals
  • You can still control cookies through other methods
