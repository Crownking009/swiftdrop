Frequently asked questions

How do I get a quote?

Quotes are computed instantly based on pickup, delivery distance and package size. Use our price calculator on the pricing page.

Can I schedule deliveries?

Yes. You can choose immediate dispatch or schedule a delivery for later today or another day.

What if recipient isn't available?

Rider will attempt contact. You can set delivery instructions in the order. We also offer return-to-sender or hold options.

Do you support business integrations?

Yes — we have APIs and batch upload options. Visit the partnerships page to learn more.
