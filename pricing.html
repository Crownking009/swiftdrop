Do I pay per trip or monthly?

Per trip by default. Businesses may choose subscription billing for volume discounts.

Are taxes included?

Prices shown exclude regulatory taxes; final checkout shows taxes where applicable.

Can I get a corporate invoice?

Yes — invoices and billing statements available for registered business accounts.
