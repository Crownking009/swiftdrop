Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about deliveries, riders, pricing and support.

Categories

How do I place an order?

Use our booking form on the site or app. Enter pickup & delivery addresses, select package size and speed, then confirm payment or choose COD where available.

Can I schedule deliveries?

Yes—choose 'Schedule' during checkout to set a date/time. We support same-day and future bookings.

What if recipient is unavailable?

Riders call recipients; you can set delivery instructions. Options: leave with neighbor, hold, or return-to-sender. Contact support for urgent cases.

How is pricing calculated?

Pricing = distance + package size/weight + service speed. Use the Pricing calculator for instant quotes.

Do you offer business discounts?

Yes—volume discounts and custom plans available. Email partnerships@swiftdrop.ng.

Is there an API?

We provide REST APIs for booking, tracking and webhooks. Request access via partnerships@swiftdrop.ng.

Can I upload orders in bulk?

Yes—CSV uploads and platform integrations available. Contact onboarding for setup.

How do I report a delivery issue?

Report via the app or Contact page. Provide order ID, rider name (if shown) and photos if applicable.

What is your response time?

Typical reply < 24 hours. Urgent hotline requests prioritized during business hours.

How are riders vetted?

Riders complete ID checks, vehicle inspection and training before activation.

What equipment must riders carry?

Riders carry ID, safety gear, secure carriers and a charged phone. Protective packaging recommended for fragile items.

How are riders paid?

Payouts processed per agreement (daily/weekly) via mobile money or bank transfer.

Are riders insured?

We provide rider liability and limited cargo insurance for qualifying services. Contact support for coverage details.

How do riders contact dispatch?

Riders use the Rider app to message dispatch or call the rider hotline for emergencies.

How is proof of delivery captured?

Proof via signature, photo or OTP—attached to order history for review.

What items are restricted?

Prohibited: hazardous materials, illegal goods, and regulated items. See Terms & Conditions or contact support for exceptions.

How do refunds & claims work?

Submit claims on Contact page with evidence. We investigate and process refunds per policy.
